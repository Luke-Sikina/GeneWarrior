Marking and Annotating a Sequence

Highlight part of your sequence by coloring it or annotate it using directed arrows. The color will be kept for certain actions such as reverse complement and translation.

Color or annotate
Select the desired part of the sequence by dragging your mouse.

The selected sequence can now be colored by clicking on the colored boxes.
To annotate the selected sequence, enter the annotation text into the text field and choose the desired arrow type.
